# Business Model Canvas (BMC)

## Value Proposition

stoch-upgrade offers a comprehensive solution for modernizing outdated stochastic infrastructure, providing a streamlined upgrade pathway that reduces manual effort, enhances performance, and minimizes risks. Our value proposition is centered around:

*   **Automated Migration**: Intelligent infrastructure assessment and upgrade planning to reduce manual effort by 80%
*   **Performance Boost**: Optimized stochastic algorithms and scalable architecture patterns to enhance system throughput by up to 40%
*   **Cost Reduction**: Streamlined deployment processes and reduced downtime to cut maintenance overhead by 60%
*   **Risk Mitigation**: Comprehensive rollback capabilities and pre-validation checks to minimize upgrade risks

## Customer Segments

stoch-upgrade is designed specifically for data science and ML engineering teams managing complex stochastic systems. Our target customer segments include:

*   **Data Science Teams**: Managing large-scale stochastic systems and seeking to modernize their infrastructure
*   **ML Engineering Teams**: Developing and deploying AI and ML models, requiring efficient and scalable stochastic infrastructure
*   **Cloud and DevOps Teams**: Responsible for maintaining and upgrading stochastic systems, seeking to reduce manual effort and minimize risks
